Goodles Mac & Cheez

Description

It'sa Mac & Cheez Time-a

Ingredients

  • 1 Box Goodle Mac & Cheese (dealer's choice)

Steps

  1. Fill appropriately sized pot with water and salt
  2. Boil water
  3. Open box
  4. Pour mac in boiled water
  5. Boil mac for several minutes
  6. Drain mac
  7. Put unwatered mac back into pot
  8. Pour powdered cheese on mac
  9. Pour some milk on mac
  10. Add some butter to pot
  11. Mix it all together
  12. Serve
