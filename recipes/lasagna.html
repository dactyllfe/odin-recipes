World's Best Lasagna



Description


Ingredients


  • 4 Italian Sausage
  • 500g Ground Beef
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 Can Crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 Cans Tomato Sauce
  • 2 Cans Tomato Paste
  • 2 TBSP Sugar
  • 1 TSP Parsley
  • 1 TBSP Basil
  • 2 TBSP Italian Seasoning
  • 1 TSP Fennell Seeds
  • 2 TSP Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • 200g Parmesan Cheese
  • 200g Mozzarella Cheese
  • 200g Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 Egg


    • Steps


    1. Heat oven to 375F
    2. Make the meat sauce
    3. Cook the noodles
    4. Make the ricotta mixture
    5. Layer the lasagna
      1. Meat sauce
      2. Noodles
      3. Ricotta mixture
      4. Mozzarella slices
      5. Meat sauce
      6. Parmesan cheese
      7. Repeat the layers
      8. Top with the remaining parmesan
    6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes
    7. Bake uncovered for 25 minutes
    8. Take out of oven and rest at room temperature for 15 minutes
